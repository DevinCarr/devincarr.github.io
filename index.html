Curriculum Innovation

At University, I am currently working with a professor to improve his Unix basics class that covers: memory hierarchy, processes, IPC, threading/synchronization, networking, and security. Our current vision is to provide the students with Raspberry Pi 2's to allow them to tinker and delve deep into the Linux operating system without worrying about IT overhead that most university servers have. To encourage curiosity, we are developing small problems that reinforce topics taught in lecture while also providing students information to explore beyond the provided material.
